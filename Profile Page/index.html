My City

I'm from Taraz and I have lived in this city for 18 years

Taraz is an ancient city of Kazakhstan with a history of 2000 years.

Taraz is a city and the administrative center of Jambyl Region in Kazakhstan, located on the Talas (Taraz) River in the south of the country near the border with Kyrgyzstan. It had a population of 330,100 (1999 Census), up 9% from 1989, making it one of the fastest-growing cities in the country, after Nur-Sultan and Turkistan.
